BATMAN

I'm introducing...Batman

Batman is a superhero in Gotham City. He seeks revenge due to his parents death and wants to protect the city. He wants to bring justice to a corrupt world. He has no superpowers, he defeats villains with his own gadgets such as batarangs. His main criminal is the Joker which a psycopath criminal.

The Dark Knight is a superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows the story of Batman as he faces one of his greatest challenges in Gotham City. The film introduces the Joker a villain, who wreaks chaos throughout the city. The movie explores themes of morality, heroism, and the blurred line between good and evil. It is known for its intense action sequences, complex characters, and gripping storytelling.
